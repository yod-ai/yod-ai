About Us

YOD-AI is a mission-driven initiative to enhance pet adoption through AI. After the 2024 legislation in Türkiye, shelters have become overcrowded. Our AI-powered platform, Köpük, simplifies the adoption process, ensuring every pet finds the perfect home.

Our Project: Köpük

We leverage AI to create comprehensive pet profiles, match adopters with suitable pets, and provide post-adoption guidance. Our platform ensures responsible pet ownership while reducing the burden on shelters.
